About me

Hi, I’m João Queirós — a Mid Software Engineer based in Lisbon, Portugal.

I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Instituto Superior Técnico (University of Lisbon), where I focused on distributed systems and microservices architecture. Over the past 2 years in the Fintech industry, I’ve applied these foundations to deliver scalable, cloud-ready solutions.

My core stack includes Java (Spring Boot and Classic Spring), Angular, Docker, Kubernetes, and Oracle DB. I work with Kafka for event-driven systems and leverage tools like GitHub Actions, Bamboo, Helm, and ArgoCD to automate CI/CD and deployment workflows. I'm also experienced with Testcontainers, JUnit 5, Mockito, and PIT for robust automated and mutation testing.

I design resilient systems using AWS (EC2, RDS), observability tools like Grafana and Jaeger, and apply architectural patterns such as Clean Architecture, Hexagonal Architecture, and Domain-Driven Design. I thrive in Agile teams, value collaboration, and enjoy tackling complex challenges with clarity and purpose.

If you're interested in connecting or collaborating on impactful projects, feel free to reach out — I’m always open to new ideas and opportunities.
